INTERNET FOR EVERYONE

Moja is a free public WiFi network from the BRCK team. Anyone within range of the signal can connect to the internet for free, or you can watch shows, listen to music or read books from the stored content on the network.

The Moja platform allows anyone to get online for free to enjoy the internet or get access to great movies, music or books.
The Moja Android app gives a faster and better experience wherever the Moja WiFi signal is found. Download the app today on the Playstore.
Music, TV shows, cartoons, and books are all types of content you can find on the Moja network, making long bus rides more enjoyable.
